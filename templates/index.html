Do not wait. Cardiac events are time-critical. Call emergency services immediately.

Warning Signs

  • Chest pressure, tightness or squeezing pain
  • Pain radiating to arm, jaw, neck or back
  • Sudden shortness of breath
  • Cold sweat, nausea or lightheadedness
  • Rapid or irregular heartbeat

If you experience 2 or more of these symptoms, call emergency services immediately. Do not drive yourself to hospital.
